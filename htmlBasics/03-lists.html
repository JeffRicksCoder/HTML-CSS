Lasagna Recipe

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Noodles to Boiling Water
  3. Heat Sauce in separate pot
  4. Lay out noodles in baking pan
  5. Add Cheese
  6. Pour on some Sauce
  7. Repeat steps 4-6
  8. Bake in oven at 375 for 2 hours
  9. Remove from oven let cool for 10 minutes before serving
